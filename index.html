Phalaenopsis violacea orchid

It is a small-to-medium-sized orchid that has beautiful purple flowers. It blooms in spring and summer, and the flowers have a spicy, cinnamon-like fragrance.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a plant that can grow in a bush or as tall as a tree. Trees of this species sometimes reach up to 15 feet high. The most commonly grown is hibiscus syriacus, or rose of Sharon, which is a perennial that is grown as a bush or tree that can get up to 12 feet tall. The plant has 679 different species of many sizes, colors, and even shapes. Most of these are perennials, but a few are annuals.
